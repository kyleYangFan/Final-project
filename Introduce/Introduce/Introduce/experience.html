Experience

Study and Life in Netherlands

I once went to the Netherlands to study for about a year, when I left home for the first time, left my parents, my friends went on a trip. I feel very lonely, but with a little excitement. When I first entered the Dutch land, I was not as excited as I expected. This piece of land is like a small township. Compared with the hometown, it only has a little more scenery and less fetters. During this study, I traveled to every corner of the city of The Hague. This is a city by the sea. The sea breeze, seabirds, and waves are washing the city every day. People here are lazy, relaxing every day, drinking coffee and talking.

Travel Life in Paris

During this time, I have also been to Paris, saying that Paris is an art city, and I look forward to it. When I arrived in Paris, I thought I had passed through the last century, except for modern cars, all in retro style. Art is a reflection of humanity. I am very lucky to be able to visit Notre Dame Cathedral. It is also fortunate to visit before it is destroyed. I also visited the Pantheon, which contains excellent paintings, sculptures of ancestors, and coffins.
